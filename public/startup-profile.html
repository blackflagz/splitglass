SplitGlass — Startup Profile

SplitGlass is a Morocco-based startup building software for construction tender intelligence.

Our product helps contractors and tender teams process Moroccan tender documents (CPS, BPU, DQE, BPDE) from PDF, DOCX, and ZIP files into bidder-ready outputs with audit checks.

Core Capabilities

  • Tender preflight analysis (go/no-go support)
  • Compliance checklist extraction (required documents, guarantees, deadlines)
  • BPU/DQE table extraction to clean Excel
  • Audit and risk flags for bid preparation

Target Customers

  • Construction SMEs
  • Bureaux d'études
  • Tender consultants
